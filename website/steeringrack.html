How To Know Your Steering Rack Need A Repair?

Difficulty Turning the Steering Wheel

If Steering wheel is harder to turn or feels stiff, it could be a sign that the steering rack is worn out or damaged. A malfunctioning rack may not allow the proper fluid flow, making steering less responsive.

Unusual Noises (Whining or Clunking)

If you hear whining, groaning, or clunking noises when turning the wheel, it may indicate problems with the steering rack or the power steering system. Leaks or worn-out components can cause these sounds.

Fluid Leaks Near the Steering Rack

fluid leakage under the car, especially near the rack area. Power steering fluid leaks can lead to a loss of pressure, affecting your vehicle's steering performance.

Uneven Tire Wear

If tires are wearing unevenly or you notice abnormal tread patterns, it could be due to a misaligned or failing steering rack. Misalignment caused by a damaged rack can lead to poor handling and premature tire wear.
