SERVICES

MAJOR AND BASIC SERVICES

Regular car maintenance, including basic and major services, ensures longevity, safety, and performance. Basic service covers oil changes and inspections, preventing minor issues, while major service replaces key components like spark plugs and brakes. Skipping maintenance can lead to costly repairs and safety risks, making regular servicing essential.
